Introducing Afya Pap

Afya Pap has a wide range of useful functionality to help our users to tackle their condition.

Personalised Health Chat

Our chatbot provides daily nutrition, excercise and mental wellness 'health tips' that are easy-to-action and tailored to your unique circumstances. Here you can find workout regimes and secret recipes tailored just for you ensuring the best possible health outcomes.

Direct-to-Doctor Communication

Keeping healthy isn't always easy to do alone. Access our Doctor's via a voice call or SMS to receive ongoing support when you need it most.

Health Insights

Record, store and track your health metrics. Users recieve a detailed breakdown of their health readings, enabling them to track their progress and understand what works for them when managing their health. Through our caregiver portal you can share this information with your Doctor in realtime, giving you peace of mind that someone has got your back.
