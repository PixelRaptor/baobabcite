Services offered via Afya Pap Medic

Primary Care

Here are some of the things we can help with:

  • Women’s health (UTI, yeast infections, menstrual problems)
  • Men’s Health (Erectile dysfunction, prostate health)
  • Skin Conditions (eczema, acne, rash)
  • Cold and Flu
  • Eye problems
  • Diarrhoea
  • Headaches
  • Vomiting
  • Fever

Prevention And Wellness

Here are some of the things we can help with:

  • Information on Screenings (obesity, depression, Diabetes, blood pressure, cancers)
  • Nutritional counselling
  • Vaccination

Chronic Care

Here are some of the things we can help with:

  • Diabetes mellitus
  • High Cholestrol
  • Hypertension
  • Depression
  • Asthma

Mental Health

Here are some of the things we can help with:

  • Stress management
  • Depression
  • Anxiety

Services Not Offered

  • Blood in stool, vomit or cough
  • Weakness or numbness on either side of body
  • Loss of consciousness
  • Difficulty Breathing
  • Traumatic injuries
  • Seizures (Fits)
  • Lacerations
  • Chest pain
  • Burns
