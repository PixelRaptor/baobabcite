Our Story

Baobab Circle Pvt Ltd is a London based multi-award-winning Health Technology Company founded in 2017 and now has operations in 10 countries across Europe, Sub-Saharan Africa and the Middle East providing personalised health care solutions and addressing the growing issue of chronic diseases. Baobab Circle uses mobile technology for remote patient monitoring and applied health signals that is powered by artificial intelligence (AI), for personalised diabetes, hypertension, meal planning and physical exercise planning and tracking. Baobab Circle has developed Afya Pap the best-in-class self-management mobile solutions to address the need for self-management of chronic conditions for improved health outcomes. Our applications are currently used by over one hundred thousand people for prevention purposes by people with high risk score of developing diabetes and reducing development of complication in those already diagnosed.
