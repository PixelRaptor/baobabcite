Our Story

Baobab Circle Ltd is a multi-award-winning Health Technology Company registered in the UK with subsidiaries across Africa. The company has operations in 7 countries across Sub-Saharan Africa and the Middle East providing personalised health care solutions and addressing the growing issue of chronic diseases. Our product, Afya Pap is a patient centred platform using AI and behavioural science to improve management of chronic conditions (e.g diabetes) by providing localised health education and personalised remote monitoring via a USSD/SMS platform and through our App. Our product enables even the most remote person to be monitored via our SMS based Ask a Doctor feature or by using our connected glucometers and blood pressure monitors. In response to COVID-19 we have expanded our offering to include triage and Dr-Patient connectivity through voice and video consultations.
